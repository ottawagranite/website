The Granite Curling Club of West Ottawa

Established in 1954 in the modern district of Westboro, the "Granite" is the center for the sport of Curling in West Ottawa.

We are always welcoming new members. Please see our getting started resources if you want to learn to curl for the first time, or just to come out and visit the club for the first time. We have an excellent rookies program for adults, a little rocks program for kids, and a bantams program for teens.

For current members, please see the members only area to manage your information, view schedules, download calendars, etc.

Thank you for visiting.
